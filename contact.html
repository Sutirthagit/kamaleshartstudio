Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission take?

Commission timelines vary based on size and complexity, typically ranging from 2-8 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in the commission price?

The price includes all materials, initial sketches, the finished artwork, professional varnishing, and secure packaging for delivery.

Can I see the artwork in progress?

Absolutely! I provide regular progress photos throughout the creation process so you can see your artwork come to life.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide with professional art packaging to ensure your artwork arrives safely. Shipping costs vary by location.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final piece?

Your satisfaction is my priority. I work closely with you throughout the process to ensure the final piece exceeds your expectations.

Can you recreate an existing artwork?

I focus on creating original, one-of-a-kind pieces. However, I can create something inspired by a particular style or theme you love.
